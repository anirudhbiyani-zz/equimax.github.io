Our Services


The team at Equimax Value Advisors have advised clients on valuation and corporate finance issues for over 15 years, with a proven track record of supporting and defending our analyses and conclusions. We offer a broad array of expert valuation opinions and advisory services that meet both market and regulatory requirements for a wide variety of purposes, including:

  • Financial Reporting - Valuations for purposes of purchase price allocation, goodwill and intangible asset impairment, M&A activity, intellectual property analysis, share based payments, fresh start accounting, litigation settlement and a variety of other financial reporting activities.

  • Accounting Reporting - Valuations in connection with a wide variety of tax planning and compliance activities, including goodwill and asset allocation to legal entities, fair value, employee stock benefit schemes, purchase price allocation and many other purposes.

  • Regulatory Reporting - Valuations for purposes of regulatory compliance related to Companies Act, Income Tax, RBI/FEMA, SEBI/Exchanges, NCLT/Courts, IBC.

Within each category, we offer a full range of valuation services and opinions to meet any need that may arise.
